## Create the Notebook instance

1. Search **Notebooks AI Platform**.
2. Click **+ NEW INSTANCE** at the top of the screen.
3. Select **Python 3**.
4. Click CREATE.

## Load the notebook

1. Click **OPEN JUPYTERLAB**.

2. Under Other, click **Terminal**.
